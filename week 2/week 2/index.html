Something that I want to learn more of is dolphins since they are one of my favorite animals. Some fun facts about dolphins is that they have 72-104 teeth! They also do not use their teeth to chew, they use them to grab food and swallow the food whole. Dolphins also get all their water from the fish they eat, they don't directly drink water. Dolphins are some of the most intelligent mammals on our planet. Dolphins communicate through chirps and whistles, they are also very playful and do jumps and acrobatic spins in the air.
